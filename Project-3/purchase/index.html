Alice's Adventures in Wonderland follows a young girl named Alice who falls down a rabbit hole into a fantastical world filled with bizarre characters and nonsensical situations. As she navigates this strange landscape, Alice encounters peculiar characters. Each encounter challenges her understanding of reality and logic, leading her on a journey of self-discovery.
